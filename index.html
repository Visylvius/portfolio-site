Throughout his extensive career, Tobias Frere-Jones has proven himself to be one of the world’s most prolific type designers. When he decided to go independent and start his own foundry, he contacted us to design and build his new website. We designed the site to direct as much attention as possible to the type itself, with the interface receding into the background. One of our main focuses was making the complex pricing system for various combinations of licenses and type styles clear and intuitive.
