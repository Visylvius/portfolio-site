Jobascript
Software Engineer

The process in searching for a software engineering job can be stressful. They're so many different places that you can search for job postings, and those sites don't let you look for jobs from your favorite company. We made Jobascript to let you do just that. Jobascript pulls job listings from three different API's, and seperates them by companies. Making it easier to find all the job postings from your favorite company. The simple UI lets you save your favorite companies, and then look for relevant news on these companies. Organizing your job search into a fun to use tool.

On The Way
Software Engineer

Have you ever been at work, and wanted to stop by a coffee shop but wanted it to be a set distance between work and home? Perhaps you wanted to hit the gym on your way to your friends house. But didn't want to stray to far out of the way? Now you have such information at your fingertips with On the Way. Simply input your two destinations and how far your willing to travel between both locations. On the Way then finds all places within the alloted time. Time efficiency made easy.
